Scrambled Eggs

Learn to make one of the easiest and most nutritious dishes on the planet!

Scrambled eggs are cherished by millions of people.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Milk

Instructions

  1. Break the eggs and mix them with a bit of milk and salt on a deep plate or in a mug.
  2. Put one tablespoon of butter in a frying pan and let it melt.
  3. Pour the eggs into the frying pan.
  4. Mix them until they are very soft and solid.

There you have it, delicious scrambled eggs.
